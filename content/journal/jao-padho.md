title: "Jao Padho: Expanding Education Access"
date: "2026-03-01"
excerpt: "How learning support initiatives are helping underserved students continue school with confidence."
---

Gridlabs Research Foundation continues to strengthen education access through **Jao Padho**, delivering practical learning support to students from underserved communities.

The initiative provides textbooks, notebooks, stationery, and uniforms in collaboration with partners and institutions. By reducing out-of-pocket burden on families, the program helps students remain enrolled and focused on learning outcomes.

As implementation grows across additional regions, impact tracking remains central to ensuring each contribution creates sustainable long-term educational benefits.
